Thank you for your participation in this research endeavor. Our survey aims to evaluate the implementation of AI technologies, such as object detection in paintings and the generation of question-answer pairs, to enhance user engagement and enrich online experiences within cultural heritage settings. Your input will offer valuable insights into how these advancements can elevate visitor interactions and inform future advancements. For optimal viewing, we suggest utilizing a desktop device, as the paintings may not be fully optimized for mobile platforms.

Embark on a captivating voyage through the Rijksmuseum, where the splendor of Dutch art awaits exploration. Engage in unraveling riddles, uncovering hidden intricacies, and delving into the narratives woven within each masterpiece. Through attentive observation, delve into the enigmatic allure concealed within every brushstroke, and reveal the treasures awaiting discovery.

GENERAL RULES:

  • Participation is completely anonymous.
  • Solve the riddle by identifying the hidden object within the painting.
  • Use the Info icon  info  in the top right to view the painting title, artist name, and description, which will aid in solving the riddle.
  • To reveal the hidden object, Click and Hold over the general area for approx 2 seconds until the object appears.
  • You can proceed to the next painting only after the hidden object is revealed.
  • Use the Hints icon  image_search  if you are unable to spot the hidden object.
  • You will solve 5 riddles generated from a combination of 11 paintings and 25 identified objects.
  • Complete a quick survey via Google Forms at the end of your session.
